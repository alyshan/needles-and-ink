Blue Lotus Tattoo Studio

Hello and Welcome to Blue Lotus Tattoo Studio. We are a Professional, Health Licensed Tattoo Studio providing high quality tattooing services. Our aim is to provide you with exceptional workmanship and world class standards, accompanied by friendly, personable service, in a safe and hygienic environment. You have the right to a great tattoo and an unforgettable experience! Our work speaks for itself!
